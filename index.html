The Toyota 4runner

The Toyota 4runner has a legendary reputation amongst off road SUV fans for the following reasons:

  1. Every 4runner since 1984 has been built in Japan
    • There have been more than 4 million 4runners build and sold!
    • They are known for their high resale value and reliability.
  2. All 4runners are built with high ground clearance and a body on frame design
    • This design creates a capable off road machine!
